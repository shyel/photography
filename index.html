Photography made me look at the world differently. It taught me not to take anything for granted and to find beauty in everything. It made me want to travel more and see new places and be more curious. I am originaly from the Philippines and now residing in Arizona, USA. I enjoy taking photos of people, nature and old buildings. My interest in photography started when I had a phone with camera. I now own a Nikon D3200 and I absolutely love it.


I hope you enjoy my photographs as much as I enjoyed taking them. Welcome to my site and have fun browsing!
